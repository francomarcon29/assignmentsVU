Reviews

Google Pixel 6

The Google Pixel 6 is a great follow up to the 5. Finally a real high-end flagship from Google, that even costs a little less than some other high-end smartphones out there.

There is a new camera in the camera bar which makes great photos partly due to Google's image software. Even though the 6 lacks a telephoto lens, it can zoom in quite far.

The OS is very fast and updates come in quickly just like we are used to from Google phones. The phone will also be supported for a long time.

The battery capacity is above average but it doesn't charge as fast as other phones in its price range.
